## Summary

This article provides a comprehensive comparison of **SRE (Site Reliability Engineering)** and **DevOps**, clarifying their origins, goals, and focuses. You'll learn how **SRE** is essentially an opinionated implementation of **DevOps**, with a strong emphasis on ensuring system **availability** and **reliability**. The article explores the roles within each approach, highlighting that **DevOps** relies on cross-functional teams, while **SRE** defines a specific engineering role. It also covers key techniques and best practices, offering valuable insights for software engineers and organizations looking to improve their software engineering practices.

The key differences between SRE and DevOps are:

*   **DevOps**: Continuous delivery and delivery speed.
*   **SRE**: Availability and reliability.
